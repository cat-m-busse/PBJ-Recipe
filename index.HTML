Simple Dessert Crepes

A step to step guide on how to prepare dessert Crepes.

This recipe makes around 12 crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups 2% milk
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt,milk, and butter until smooth.
  2. In a separate bolw, beat eggs until blended and smooth.
  3. Pour eggs into first bowl, and mix together. (Be careful not to mix too well)
  4. Let mixture sit for at least 30 minutes in refridgerator.
  5. Heat a pan on stove on medium setting. lightly coat with butter.
  6. Using a ladle, pour a light amount of batter into pan. (You don't want it too thick or it'll turn out pancake-like)
  7. Make sure to spread out batter equally on pan before it cooks. You want it to cook evenly.
  8. Cook crepe until edges start to lift from pan, and bottom starts to become golden. This should take around 1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Carefully flip crepe with spatula when ready.
  9. Cook crepe on second side until set and golden on bottom. This should take about 45-50 seconds.
  10. Repeat steps 6-9 until batter runs out. Let crepes cool to room temperature before serving.
